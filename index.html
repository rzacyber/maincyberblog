The End of QakBot

In a multinational effort involving the United States, France, Germany, the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, Romania, and Latvia, the Justice Department targeted the Qakbot botnet and its malware, dismantling its infrastructure. Qakbot's malicious code is being removed from victim computers, halting further damage. Additionally, the Department seized over $8.6 million in cryptocurrency, representing illicit profits. This operation is the most extensive U.S.-led intervention against a cybercriminal botnet, impacting ransomware attacks, financial fraud, and other cybercrimes.

Types of Networks

Computer networks come in various types, including Ethernet (wired LAN), WLAN (Wi-Fi), WAN (Wide Area Network), and Cloud Networking. Ethernet offers fast, wired connections but can be challenging to upgrade. WLAN allows mobile devices but is less secure. WAN connects multiple local networks but is harder to secure. Cloud Networking is gaining popularity for its versatility, speed, and cost-effectiveness but requires an internet connection and is a target for cyberattacks. Choose your network type wisely based on your needs and security considerations.
